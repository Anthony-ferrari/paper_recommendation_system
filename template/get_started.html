Getting Started...


Busca Papeles is a website made for people looking to find similar research articles based on the one they upload onto our site.

  1. You can check to see if the research article you currently have is in our database. If it is, then select it and click on process to get the list of research articles similar to the one selected.
  2. You can make save any article in the results lists by clicking on the save button after you create an account with us. You will be able to see your saved articles in the "My Data" tab.
